    题干

    \[ \begin{vmatrix} a_{0} & a_{1} & \cdots & a_{n} \\ a_{1} & a_{2} & \cdots & a_{0} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a_{n} & a_{0} & \cdots & a_{n-1} \end{vmatrix} \]

    答案

    令\(f(x)=\sum_{k=0}^{n}a_{k}x^k\),记\(\varepsilon=e^{\frac{2\pi i}{n+1}}\)为1的\(n+1\)次单位根，注意到: 提示，\(e^{\pi i}=-1\)

    \[ \begin{bmatrix} a_{0} & a_{1} & \cdots & a_{n} \\ a_{1} & a_{2} & \cdots & a_{0} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a_{n} & a_{0} & \cdots & a_{n-1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\ 1 & \varepsilon & \cdots & \varepsilon^{n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ 1^{n} & \varepsilon^{n} & \cdots & \varepsilon^{n^{2}} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} f(1) & f(\varepsilon) & \cdots & f(\varepsilon^{n}) \\ f(1) & \varepsilon^{-1}f(\varepsilon) & \cdots & \varepsilon^{-n}f(\varepsilon^{n})\\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ f(1) & \varepsilon^{-n}f(\varepsilon) & \cdots & \varepsilon^{-n^{2}}f(\varepsilon^{n}) \end{bmatrix} \]

    行数与列数错开越来越大，使得需要通过提出 \(\varepsilon^{-n}\) 使得含\(a_{n}\)的多项式得以符合 \(f(\varepsilon)\) 的形式

    在这一过程中，通过 \(\varepsilon\) \(n+1\) 次单位根的设置，使一些在提出 \(\varepsilon^{-n}\) 后的项(比如 \(a_{0}\)) 的系数得以变回其原本的系数

    接着，两边取行列式

    \[ \begin{vmatrix} a_{0} & a_{1} & \cdots & a_{n} \\ a_{1} & a_{2} & \cdots & a_{0} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a_{n} & a_{0} & \cdots & a_{n-1} \end{vmatrix} \times D(1,\varepsilon,\varepsilon^{2},\cdots,\varepsilon^{n}) = D(1,\varepsilon^{-1},\varepsilon^{-2},\cdots,\varepsilon^{n}) \prod_{k=0}^{n}f(\varepsilon^{k}) \]
